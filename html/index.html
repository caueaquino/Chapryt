About:

Chapryt is a program that solve the problem between users on chat. It is a encrypted chat that provide secure to user's messages and their data, also grants privacy to users. Chapryt was developed by students of UTFPR oriented by teacher Lucas Dias Hiera Sampaio for the subject of security and systems auditing in the second half of 2018.
